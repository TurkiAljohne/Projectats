Group Fitness

Body Masters Group Fitness Classes are designed to help you raise your fitness level, lose weight and reshape your body, our Group Fitness Instructors are well experienced and highly qualified from renowned fitness academic organizations to help you achieve your personal health and fitness goals while enjoying the classes.



General Fitness Class:

This class focuses on stimulating blood circulation to all body parts using
the great combination of aerobic, endurance and strength exercises
designed to help you reshape your body and raise your overall fitness level



Step Class:

Sculpting your core/hips and strengthening your legs in one of our strongest
classes, designed to be easy to perform and help you to upgrade your
fitness level in a fun and enjoyable session.



Spinning Class:

It’s a challenge of endurance… Fun and enthusiastic experience to raise the
ability of your heart and lungs while burning more calories, there’s no
complicated moves to learn, just ride your bike and enjoy the enthusiastic
environment with the group and professional instructor.



Body Fit Class:

Using the Barbells and Dumbbells, challenge your body and enjoy our
variety of exercises to all your body parts, as little as 45 minutes a day
guarantees you strength, endurance, and fat loss.
